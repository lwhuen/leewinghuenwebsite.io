Make my own Perfume

Perfume notes are separated into three-notes: top notes, middle notes, and base notes. Top notes give the impression of fragrance to the people, usually using citrus or herbal essential oil. The duration of top notes is about 5 to 15 minutes. Middle notes are the main scent style, like floral or woody fragrances. It stays 20 to 60 minutes. Base Notes are the foundation of the perfume. The fragrance will sustain our bodies for more than six hours. The ingredient of perfumes are essential oils and alcohol.
